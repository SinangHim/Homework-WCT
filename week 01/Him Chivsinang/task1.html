1.<!DOCTYPE html>

        You'll need this tag at the begining of every HTML document you create. It ensures that a 
        browser know that it's reading HTML, and that it expect HTML5,the latest version.
        
        Even though this isn't actually an HTML tag, it's still good one to know.

2. <html>

        This is another tag that tell a browser that it's reading HTML. The <html> tag goes
        straight after the DOCTYPE tag , and you close it with a <html>tag right at the end of your 
        file.Everything else in your document goes between these tags.
